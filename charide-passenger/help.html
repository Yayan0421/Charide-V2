Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ride?

Click on "Book a Ride" from your dashboard, enter your pickup and destination locations, select your vehicle type, and confirm the booking.

How can I track my driver?

Once your ride is confirmed, go to "Track Driver" to see your driver's real-time location and estimated arrival time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets, and cash payments. You can manage your payment method in your account settings.

How do I report a safety issue?

If you experience any safety concerns, please contact our support team immediately at support@charide.com or call +639-CHARIDE.
