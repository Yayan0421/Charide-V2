About Cha Ride

ESSU Campus Transport System

Overview

Cha Ride is a campus transportation system designed to help students, staff, and teachers move around the ESSU campus safely and conveniently. It connects passengers with available tricycle and motorcycle drivers, making campus travel faster and more organized.

Objectives

  • Provide safe and convenient campus transportation
  • Reduce waiting time for passengers
  • Organize and manage campus transport efficiently

Key Features

  • Ride request and tracking
  • Driver availability monitoring
  • Fare and payment recording
  • Admin dashboard for system management

System Users

  • Passengers: Students, staff, and teachers
  • Drivers: Tricycle and motorcycle drivers
  • Admin: Manages system operations

Benefits

  • Faster and safer campus travel
  • Transparent and reliable fare management
  • Improved experience for passengers and drivers
  • Organized campus transportation system
